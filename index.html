COVID-19 Cases from 2020 to 2023

The d3.js data visualization project

Jianyang Wu

This page presents an interactive slideshow to provide a comprehensive view of the COVID-19 worldwide influences.

The utilized dataset includes infection cases, deaths through 2020 to 2023, with also various derived accumulated data.

An Overview of COVID-19 in U.S.

Scene 1

In this overview, you will see the changes of COVID-19 from 2020 to 2023 in the states with top 5 populations.
You can also opt a single state in the chart, with detailed statistics of COVID-19 cases and deaths when hovering on the circle. A slider is provided to move to a certain date and the data of the states on that day will show in the chart.

COVID-19 Cases & Deaths in All States in U.S.

Scene 3
